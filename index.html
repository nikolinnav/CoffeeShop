Simply Good Coffee

Small-batch roasts, simple food, and a calm space in the heart of the city.

"Stillroom Coffee was founded to create a calm and welcoming space for coffee lovers. We believe every cup deserves time to be savored, whether alone or with friends. Our beans are sourced from small, sustainable farms and roasted in-house, ensuring rich flavors and ethical practices. Every step is done with care, from farm to cup. We also believe in simplicity — our menu focuses on quality over quantity, offering carefully selected coffees, teas, and fresh pastries. Every item on the menu is designed to complement the experience of your perfect cup."
